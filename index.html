The sport of hockey has been my biggest passion since I first stepped on the ice when I was 4 years old. I have been all over the world playing hockey: From the age of 11 onwards, my father would send me abroad during the entire summer so I could play hockey and learn english at the same time. This meant that I was travelling far away from home to countries like the Czech Republic, France, USA or Canada. At the age of 14, while participating at a summer training camp in Aurora, Ontario (Canada), I piked the interest of the boarding school (Saint Andrew's College) the camp was being held at.
